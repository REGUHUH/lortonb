The Old Alarm

Bryce Lorton

Groaning stained bricks expand and contract, accelerating the aching of age. Boarded and barred windows keep festering secrets restrained. A once-vigilant burglar alarm is the face of this dying structure. The security feature was a weary old thing, never updated or retired. The ever-changing seasons and the self-grooming trees grow envy in the rusting box. Long since losing functionality, the decaying alarm box waits out its slow unassisted demise, forgotten by the world.
